##  Synopsis

*The moon hangs low and full over a sleepy French village, casting long shadows between the half-timbered houses. Somewhere in the darkness, a howl echoes through the misty cobblestone streets. The villagers bar their doors and huddle by the fire, but they know—no lock can keep out what walks on two legs by day and four by night.*

*A chill wind carries whispers of betrayal and suspicion. Neighbour turns against neighbour as fear takes root. Is that farmer hiding his livestock—or his true nature? Does the herbalist tend to the sick, or mark them for the kill? In this village, the greatest threat may not be the wolf at the door, but the one that sleeps in the bed beside you.*

*Welcome to... **Loup Garou**.*

##  Gameplay

Loup Garou is a custom homebrew scenario inspired by the classic French party game *Loup Garou de Thiercelieux*. This scenario introduces a lovesick twist on demon-hunting, where alliances shift and romance proves deadly.

The good team must identify and execute the Werewolf before it picks off the entire village. With the Seer gathering intelligence, the Witch holding the power of life and death, and the Hunter seeking vengeance from beyond the grave, the villagers have tools to fight back. But Cupid's tangled web of lovers means every death carries twice the weight—and the Thief may be wearing a friendly face while plotting betrayal.

In Loup Garou, information is your sharpest weapon. Trust no one, question everything, and remember: even the most unlikely villager may have fangs.
