##  Synopsis

*The village sleeps, unaware of the danger that stalks the moonlit streets. But tonight, the danger is closer than ever—for the wolves walk among them, hiding in plain sight. By morning, someone will be dead, and the survivors must unmask the monster before the next moonrise.*

*In this fast-paced variant, there is only one night of killing, one chance for the wolves to strike. The Seer may glimpse a soul, the Hunter may take someone down with them, and the Witch may brew salvation or doom—but when dawn breaks, the village must decide who to hang based on whispers, half-truths, and gut instinct.*

*Welcome to... **Loup Garou pour 1 nuit**.*

##  Gameplay

Loup Garou pour 1 nuit (One Night Werewolf) adapts the classic social deduction game into a single, rapid round. The game follows the same core rules as Loup Garou, but with a crucial difference: only one night occurs before the village must execute a suspect.

This scenario can be expanded with the **Crépuscule** extension, which adds mysterious characters like the Loup Mystique, Apprenti Sbire, and other twilight-dwelling entities that blur the line between village and wolf even further.

Due to the single-night format, information roles are especially powerful—but so are the wolves' abilities to mislead. Trust nothing, question everything, and remember: the night is short, but the lies can last forever.
