##  Synopsis

*The moon hangs red and swollen over the village, a bloodshot eye watching the terrified inhabitants below. The dead have not stayed dead. From shadowy corners and locked cellars, creatures of the night emerge—pale, elegant, and ravenous.*

*The Vampire walks among the villagers, charming and deadly. Its spawn—the Assassin, the Chauve-souris, the Renégat—spread chaos and fear. But hope remains: the Chasseur de Vampires hunts with holy water and stake, and the Prêtre wields faith as a weapon. In this battle between the living and the undead, only one side will see the dawn.*

*Welcome to... **Vampire pour 1 nuit**.*

##  Gameplay

Vampire pour 1 nuit reimagines the one-night format with gothic horror. The Vampire leads a coven of night creatures against a village that must unite to survive.

The vampire team (Vampire, Assassin, Apprenti Assassin, Chauve-souris, Le Comte, Renégat) work together to eliminate the village. The good team (Chasseur de Vampires, Greffier, Prêtre, Victime) must identify and stake the vampire before sunrise.

Like all one-night variants, only a single night of killing occurs before the village must execute. Information is scarce, deception is rampant, and the undead have many tricks up their tattered sleeves.
