##  Summary
"The first time you would die, you don't. If you survive this way, you lose your ability."

The Elder has survived many winters, and death itself seems reluctant to claim them.
* The first time the Elder would be killed by the Werewolf or executed, they survive instead.
* After surviving, the Elder loses their special resilience—they can die normally from then on.
* The Elder does not know when their protection is used until the Storyteller informs them.
* The Elder remains alive after their protection triggers.

##  How to Run

If the Elder would die (by execution or by the Werewolf), announce that they survive. Privately inform the Elder that their ability has been used. Mark the Elder with the **NO ABILITY** reminder. From now on, the Elder can die normally.

##  Examples

{{< example >}}
The Elder is nominated and executed. The Storyteller announces that the Elder survives. The Elder learns their ability is spent.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The Werewolf kills the Elder on night three. The Elder survives. On night four, the Werewolf kills the Elder again. This time, the Elder dies.
{{< /example >}}
