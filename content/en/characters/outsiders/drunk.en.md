##  Summary

**You do not know you are the **Drunk**.
You think you are a Townsfolk character, but you are not.**

The **Drunk** player thinks that they are a Townsfolk, and has no idea that they are actually the Drunk.

* During setup, the **Drunk**'s card does not go in the bag.
  Instead, a Townsfolk character card goes in the bag,
  and the player who draws that card is secretly the **Drunk** for the whole game.
  The Storyteller knows.
  The player does not.
* The **Drunk** has no ability.
  Whenever their Townsfolk ability would affect the game in some way, it doesn't.
  However, the Storyteller pretends that the player is the Townsfolk they think they are.
  If that character would wake at night, the **Drunk** wakes to act as if they are that Townsfolk.
  If that Townsfolk would gain information,
  the Storyteller may give them _false_ information instead

  * and the Storyteller is encouraged to do so.

##  How to Run

While setting up the game, before putting character cards in the bag,
remove the **Drunk** card and add a Townsfolk character card.

Add the Drunk's **IS THE DRUNK** reminder token to the Grimoire.
Put the swapped Townsfolk character card in the bag, not the **Drunk** character card.

While preparing the first night, put the **Drunk**'s **IS THE DRUNK** reminder token by any Townsfolk character card,
changing that player's character to the Drunk.
They are now an Outsider, and do not have the ability of this Townsfolk character.
_(But they think they do.)_

During the game, act as if the **Drunk** is actually this Townsfolk character.
_(If that character would wake to act at night, the Drunk wakes to act.
If their ability would give them information,
you can give false information to them if you wish.
See «[Drunkenness and Poisoning](/en/rulebook/states/#drunkenness-and-poisoning)» in the rulebook.)_

##  Examples

{{< example >}}
The **Drunk**, who thinks they are the [Soldier](/en/characters/townsfolk/soldier),
is attacked by the [Imp](/en/characters/demons/imp).
The Drunk dies.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The **Drunk**, who thinks they are the [Empath](/en/characters/townsfolk/empath),
wakes and learns a _"0”_ even though they are sitting next to one evil player.
The next night, they learn a _"1"_.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The **Drunk**, who thinks they are the [Ravenkeeper](/en/characters/townsfolk/ravenkeeper), is killed at night.
They choose the [Saint](/en/characters/outsiders/saint),
but learn that this player is the [Poisoner](/en/characters/minions/poisoner).
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The [Fortune Teller](/en/characters/townsfolk/fortune-teller) is executed.
That night, the **Drunk**, who thinks they are [Undertaker](/en/characters/townsfolk/undertaker),
learns that the **Drunk** _died_ today.
{{< /example >}}
