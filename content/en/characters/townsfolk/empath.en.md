##  Summary

**Each night, you learn how many of your 2 alive neighbors are evil.**

The Empath keeps learning if their living neighbours are good or evil.

* The Empath only learns how many of their neighbours are evil, not which one is evil.
* The Empath does not detect dead players.
  So, if the Empath is sitting next to a dead player, they do not get info about that dead player.
  Instead, they get info about the closest alive player in that direction.
* The Empath acts after the Demon, so if the Demon kills one of the Empath's alive neighbours,
  the Empath does not learn about the now-dead player.
  The Empath's information is accurate at dawn, not at dusk.

##  How to Run

Each night, wake the Empath.
Show them fingers (0, 1, or 2) equaling the number of evil players neighbouring the Empath.
Put the Empath to sleep.

## Quote

*My skin prickles. Something is not right here. I can feel it.*

##  Examples

{{< example >}}
The Empath neighbours two good players

* a [Soldier](/en/characters/townsfolk/soldier) and a [Monk](/en/characters/townsfolk/monk).

The Empath learns a '0'.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The next day, the [Soldier](/en/characters/townsfolk/soldier) is executed.
That night, the [Monk](/en/characters/townsfolk/monk) is killed by the [Imp](/en/characters/demons/imp).

The Empath now detects the players sitting next to the [Soldier](/en/characters/townsfolk/soldier) and the [Monk](/en/characters/townsfolk/monk),
which are a [Librarian](/en/characters/townsfolk/librarian) and an evil [Gunslinger](/en/characters/travellers/gunslinger).
The Empath now learns a '1'.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
There are only three players left alive, the Empath, the [Imp](/en/characters/demons/imp), and the [Baron](/en/characters/minions/baron).
No matter who is seated where, the Empath learns a '2'.
{{< /example >}}
