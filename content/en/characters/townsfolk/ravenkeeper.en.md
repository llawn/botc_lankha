##  Summary

**If you die at night, you are woken to choose a player: you learn their character.**

If the Ravenkeeper dies at night, they get to learn one player's character.

* The Ravenkeeper is woken on the night that they die, and chooses a player immediately.
* The Ravenkeeper may choose a dead player if they wish.

##  How to Run

If the Ravenkeeper died tonight, wake them.
They point at any player.
Show the chosen player's character card to the Ravenkeeper.
Put the Ravenkeeper to sleep.

We advise you to discourage or even ban players from talking about what they
are doing at night as they are doing it.

## Quote

_My birds will avenge me! Fly! Fly, my sweet and dutiful pets!
To the manor and to the river!
To the alleys and to the salons! Fly!_

##  Examples

{{< example >}}
The Ravenkeeper is killed by the [Imp](/en/characters/demons/imp),
and then wakes to choose a player.
After some deliberation, they choose Benjamin.
Benjamin is the [Empath](/en/characters/townsfolk/empath),
and the Ravenkeeper learns this.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The [Imp](/en/characters/demons/imp) attacks the [Mayor](/en/characters/townsfolk/mayor).
The [Mayor](/en/characters/townsfolk/mayor) doesn't die,
but the Ravenkeeper dies instead, due to the [Mayor](/en/characters/townsfolk/mayor)'s ability.
The Ravenkeeper is woken and chooses Douglas, who is a _dead_ [Recluse](/en/characters/outsiders/recluse).
The Ravenkeeper learns that Douglas is the [Scarlet Woman](/en/characters/minions/scarlet-woman),
since the [Recluse](/en/characters/outsiders/recluse) registered as a Minion.
{{< /example >}}
