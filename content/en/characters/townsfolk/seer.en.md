##  Summary
"Each night*, choose a player: you learn their character."

The Seer peers into the soul of a chosen player and learns their true nature.
* Each night except the first, the Seer wakes and chooses a living player.
* The Storyteller shows the Seer the character token of that player.
* The information is true unless the Seer is drunk or poisoned.
* The Seer learns the exact character, not just alignment.

##  How to Run

Each night except the first, wake the Seer. They point to any living player. Show the character token of that player to the Seer. Put the Seer to sleep.

##  Examples

{{< example >}}
The Seer chooses Marie on night two. Marie is the Werewolf. The Storyteller shows the Werewolf token to the Seer.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
The Seer chooses Pierre, who is the Guard. The Storyteller shows the Guard token. Pierre is confirmed good.
{{< /example >}}
