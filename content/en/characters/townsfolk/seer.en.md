##  Summary
"Each night*, choose a player: you learn their character."

The Seer peers into the soul of a chosen player and learns their true nature.
* Each night except the first, the Seer wakes and chooses a living player.
* The Storyteller shows the Seer the character token of that player.
* The information is true unless the Seer is drunk or poisoned.
* The Seer learns the exact character, not just alignment.

##  How to Run

Each night except the first, wake the Seer. They point to any living player. Show the character token of that player to the Seer. Put the Seer to sleep.

##  Examples

				The Seer chooses Marie on night two. Marie is the Werewolf. The Storyteller shows the Werewolf token to the Seer.

				The Seer chooses Pierre, who is the Guard. The Storyteller shows the Guard token. Pierre is confirmed good.

##  Tips & Tricks

* The Seer is one of the most powerful information roles in the game. When you learn a player's character, you know exactly what they should be doing. Cross-reference their claims with your knowledge.
* Watch for behavioural tells. A player who claims to be the Guard but isn't protecting anyone may be hiding something.
* Use your information to build trust networks. Once you confirm a good player, you can work together to unmask the evil team.
* Be careful revealing your information too early. The Werewolf will want you dead as soon as they know who you are.

##  Bluffing as the Seer

When bluffing as the Seer, name specific but believable characters for the players you "investigate." If you claim a player is the Werewolf and they get executed but the game doesn't end, your bluff is exposed.
