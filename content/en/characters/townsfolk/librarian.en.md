##  Summary

**You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Outsider. (Or that zero are in play.)**

The Librarian learns that a particular Outsider character is in play, but not exactly which player it is.

* During the first night, the Librarian learns that one of two players is a specific Outsider.
* They learn this only once and then learn nothing more.
* The [Drunk](/en/characters/outsiders/drunk) is an Outsider. If the Librarian learns that one of two players is the Drunk, they do not learn the Townsfolk that the Drunk's player thinks they are.

##  How to Run

While preparing the first night, put the Librarian's **OUTSIDER** reminder token by any Outsider character card,
and put the Librarian's **WRONG** reminder token by any other character card.

During the first night, wake the Librarian and point to the players marked **OUTSIDER** and **WRONG**. Show the character token marked **OUTSIDER** to the Librarian. Put the Librarian to sleep. Remove the Librarian's reminder tokens when convenient.

## Quote

*Certainly madam, under normal circumstances, you may borrow the Codex Malificarium from the library vaults. However, you do not seem to be a member*

##  Examples

{{< example >}}
Benjamin is the [Saint](/en/characters/outsiders/saint), and Filip is the [Baron](/en/characters/minions/baron).
The Librarian learns that either Benjamin or Filip is the [Saint](/en/characters/outsiders/saint).
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
There are no Outsiders in this game.
The Librarian learns a '0'.
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
Abdallah is the [Drunk](/en/characters/outsiders/drunk),
who thinks they are the [Monk](/en/characters/townsfolk/monk),
and Douglas is the [Undertaker](/en/characters/townsfolk/undertaker).
The Librarian learns that either Abdallah or Douglas is the [Drunk](/en/characters/townsfolk/drunk).
(This happens because the Librarian learns the **true** character.
The Drunk is Abdallah’s **true** character, not the Monk.)
{{< /example >}}
