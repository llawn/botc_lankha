##  Summary

**You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Minion.**

The Investigator learns that a particular Minion character is in play, but not exactly which player it is.

* During the first night, the Investigator learns that one of two players is a specific Minion.
* They learn this only once and then learn nothing more.

##  How to Run

While preparing the first night, put the Investigator's **MINION** reminder token by any Minion character card,
and put the Investigator's **WRONG** reminder token by any other character token.

During the first night, wake the Investigator and point to the players marked **MINION** and **WRONG**.
Show the character card marked **MINION** to the Investigator.
Put the Investigator to sleep.
Remove the Investigator's reminder tokens when convenient.

## Quote

*It is a fine night for a stroll, wouldn't you say, Mister Morozov?
Or should I say... BARON Morozov?*

{{< example >}}
Amy is the [Baron](/en/characters/minions/baron),

{{< example >}}

and Lewis is the [Poisoner](/en/characters/minions/poisoner).
The Investigator learns that either Angelus or Lewis is the [Spy](/en/characters/minions/spy).
{{< /example >}}

{{< example >}}
Brianna is the [Recluse](/en/characters/outsiders/recluse),
and Marianna is the [Imp](/en/characters/demons/imp).
The Investigator learns that either Brianna or Marianna is the [Poisoner](/en/characters/minions/poisoner).
(This happens because the [Recluse](/en/characters/outsiders/recluse) is registering as a Minion—in this case, the [Poisoner](/en/characters/minions/poisoner).)
